FAQs

What’s the cheapest time to fly?

Prices move with demand. Use the calendar to scan a whole month—shoulder weeks outside peak holidays often dip the most.

Which London and Bangkok airports can I choose?

London departures commonly include Heathrow (LHR), Gatwick (LGW), Stansted (STN), Luton (LTN) and City (LCY). Bangkok uses Suvarnabhumi (LON) and Don Mueang (DMK).

How early should I book?

There’s no universal rule, but booking several weeks ahead for economy is typical. If you’re flexible, watch for dips on mid-week dates.
