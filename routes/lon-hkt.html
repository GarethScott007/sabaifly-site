Quick facts

  • Airports: London (LHR/LGW/STN/LTN/LCY) → Phuket (HKT)
  • Typical duration: ~14–17h with one stop (via BKK/DOH/DXB/SIN)
  • Best months: Nov–Mar (drier high season)
  • Tip: Compare open-jaw: fly to HKT, return from BKK (or the reverse) to mix beach & city.

Price tips for LON → HKT

  • Mid-week departures (Tue–Thu) usually undercut weekends.
  • 1-stop via Gulf/SE-Asia hubs often beats indirect multi-stop combos once you add bags.
  • Use the calendar to aim just before/after peak holiday weeks for softer prices.
